About the Photography Society

The Photography Society at NIT Manipur is a vibrant community of student photographers passionate about capturing the world through their lenses. Founded in 2025, our club welcomes everyone from beginners to advanced photographers.

We believe in learning through practice and collaboration. Our members gain hands-on experience in various photography genres including portrait, landscape, street, event, macro, and architectural photography.

Whether you're looking to pursue photography professionally or simply enjoy it as a hobby, our society provides the resources, guidance, and community to help you grow as a photographer.
